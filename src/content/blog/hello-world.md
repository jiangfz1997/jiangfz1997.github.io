Every developer eventually starts a blog. This is mine.

## What this is for

I learn best by writing. Explaining a concept forces me to understand it at a level that passively reading never does. So the primary audience here is actually future me — when I've forgotten how something works and need a reference I can trust.

If it helps you too, great.

## What I'll write about

Mostly:
- **ML systems** — building, evaluating, and debugging LLM-powered applications in production
- **Engineering notes** — patterns I find useful, tools I like, mistakes I've made
- **Reading notes** — summaries and reactions to papers or books

I won't commit to a schedule. Posts when I have something worth saying.

## The stack

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and hosted on GitHub Pages. Posts are Markdown files in the repo. Nothing fancy — the goal is low friction to publish.
